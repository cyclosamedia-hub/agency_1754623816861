The Story Behind Our Success

ModernBiz Pro was founded in 2012 when our CEO, Sarah Mitchell, identified a critical gap in the consulting market. Traditional firms were delivering generic solutions, while businesses needed tailored strategies that addressed their unique challenges.

Starting with a small team of industry veterans, we developed our proprietary methodology that combines data-driven insights with human-centered design. Our first client, a struggling manufacturing company, achieved a 340% ROI increase within 8 months—setting the foundation for our reputation.
